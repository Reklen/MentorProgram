Mentor ProgramProject-based, short-term mentor sessions for learning & teaching code in byte-sized peices.

Become a Mentor Find a Mentor

This mentor program is an experiment in using short-term & project-based mentoring to make each session as focused & productive as possible. You can sign up to mentor or be mentored for three sessions, after that, you can sign up again, or take a break and come back when you can.

Check out the session outlines & project ideas to help guide mentors & mentees to get the most out of each session on the Project & Session Prep page. Or get the at-a-glance Mentor Session Guidelines & Cheatsheet and jump right in!
