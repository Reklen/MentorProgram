The goal here is to help mentors & mentees get as much out of each session as possible.
We welcome your input & ideas help to make these outlines & project ideas truely awesome! If you have an idea for a new project or session topic (or edits to any existing ones), email it to mentors@hatsnpants.com and it will get added!
